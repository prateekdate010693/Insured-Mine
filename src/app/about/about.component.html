Insured Mine

Ever since the launch of its first collection, Insured Mine's design idea becomes immediately clear: the blending of different and sometimes even apparently contrasting aesthetic Universes. Insured Mine's research focuses on designing and manufacturing the original Instant Classic, synonymous of a contemporary style that withstands the wear and tear of time.

  • FOUNDED
  • 2008
  • ORIGIN
  • Milan

FLAG - SHIPSTORE

Located in the heart of the city, the store is realized with a gradient of different marbles and zinc-coated displays reflecting the concept of Instant Classic that accompanies the brand over the years. The seasonal collection, our selection of indipendent press and the various in-store events are hosted in a space which appears bright and amplified by a mirrored wall and by the extrusion of the cashier desk that creates a continuity with the floor.
